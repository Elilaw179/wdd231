Discover Timbuktu

Explore the beauty and attractions of Timbuktu. Here are some must-visit places:

Djinguereber Mosque

Timbuktu, Mali

One of the oldest mosques in Africa, built in 1327.

Sankore University

Timbuktu, Mali

One of the three ancient centers of learning in Timbuktu.

Timbuktu Manuscripts

Timbuktu Libraries, Mali

Ancient texts showcasing African knowledge in science, astronomy, and medicine.

Ahmed Baba Institute

Timbuktu, Mali

A research center preserving thousands of manuscripts from the 13th century.

Flamme de la Paix

Timbuktu, Mali

A monument symbolizing peace, where over 3,000 firearms were burned in 1996.

Timbuktu Market

Downtown Timbuktu, Mali

A bustling market where you can find local crafts, spices, and traditional Tuareg jewelry.

Lake Faguibine

Timbuktu Region, Mali

Once the largest lake in Mali, providing water and life to the region.

Tuareg Desert Camp

Sahara Desert near Timbuktu

Experience Tuareg culture, camel rides, and stunning desert landscapes.
